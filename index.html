Our Rallies

We organise Marathon, Challenge and Carrera categories which are competative. The global tournament will take place in five countries (United Kingdom, Ireland, Norway, Denmark and Netherlands). This tournament will allow only vintage cars to participate in and it will start on Oct,31,2022.

  • Location: United Kingdom, Ireland, Norway, Denmark, Netherlands
  • Rules: Only drive on the right route
  • Discount: 20% discount for first (3) registered rallies.
  • Winner: £50,000
  • 1st Runner up: £40,000
  • 2nd Runner up: £30,000
